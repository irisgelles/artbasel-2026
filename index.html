Know before you go

The fair
decoded

Art Basel is not just an exhibition. It's a marketplace, a networking event, a cultural stage, and a status ecosystem — all compressed into one week in Miami.

The six sectors

Galleries The main floor — established galleries, major blue-chip names
Nova Newer, fresher, more experimental — often the most exciting
Positions Solo presentations by emerging artists — where you spot tomorrow's names
Survey Historical or rediscovered artists — often underrated and surprising
Kabinett Curated mini-exhibitions inside individual gallery booths
Meridians Large-scale installations, video, sculpture, performance — don't skip this

How to talk to a gallery

"Can you help me understand what I should notice in this work?"
"Is this a new body of work or an older series?"
"What would I miss if I only looked for a minute?"
"Where has this artist shown before?"
